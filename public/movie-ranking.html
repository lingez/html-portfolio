The Best Movies According to Deadfool

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Joker

One of my most favourite origin villain story. It's interesting to see how one's delusional thoughts
and being bullied drove a man to become who he is now known as the Joker. His ways may very well be
questionable but why he became what he became is perfectly reasonable but yet I do not condone that.

Batman: The Dark Knight

One of the best movies Christian Bale has acted so far and Heath Ledger is truly a legendary Joker
in this movie. The quotes in this movie by the Joker is relatable and just love how the entire movie
was directed by Christopher Nolan.

Spiderman: No Way Home

This may be a very brief reason as to why I love this movie and it's simply because I grew up watching
Toby McGuire as Spiderman and to see him make an appearance is very, very nostalgic. The movie
as a whole is well taken and has touched my heart.
